The Melon Bowl

your pocket menu for when you don't know what to eat

  • 15g powdered peanut butter (PB2)
  • 30g oat powder (blended oats)
  • 8g honey (or non-sugar syrup)
  • 150g non-fat greek yogurt
  • 30g berries (e.g., raspberries, strawberries)
  • Dark chocolate square (optional)
  1. Measure all ingredients and put in bowl.
  2. Mix.
  3. Top with berries of choice and chocolate!
