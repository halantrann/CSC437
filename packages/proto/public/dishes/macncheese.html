Mac and Cheese

Dish Properties

Type of Meal
Lunch
Cuisine
American
Taste
Umami
Calories
450 kcal
Utensil Needed
Fork
Prep Time
30 min
Time to Cook
30 min
Ingredients
  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs

Instructions

  1. Boil macaroni until al dente.
  2. Make cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, and cheddar.
  3. Mix macaroni with sauce, top with breadcrumbs, and bake.
